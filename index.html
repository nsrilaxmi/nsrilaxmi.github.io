Reproducible reanalysis of allergen-challenged airway single-cell RNA-seq data, focused on epithelial remodeling, immune signatures, sample-level composition, and pseudo-bulk differential expression.

Cancer TME

GSE131907 Lung Adenocarcinoma TME Reanalysis

Sample-aware reanalysis of a public lung adenocarcinoma scRNA-seq atlas, including tissue-site composition, expression-based tumor microenvironment signatures, subtype summaries, and paired-patient sensitivity checks.

  • 208k cells
  • 58 samples
  • Signature scoring
  • Paired sensitivity checks
Repository Figures Tables Results summary
Workflow steps
  1. Download GEO annotation and UMI matrix
  2. Build sample-aware metadata
  3. Summarize tissue-site composition
  4. Stream signature genes
  5. Run contrasts and sensitivity checks

Question

Why do two patients with the same diagnosis sometimes show completely different immune-cell programs?

Short answer: diagnosis groups can hide differences in genetics, exposures, treatment history, tissue sampling, and cell-state composition.
